How to Use

Use the 400 Blush Brush to blend powder blushes evenly for a natural yet, buildable flush. Use the 300 Deluxe Crease Brush to blend cream and powder eyeshadows evenly- or, use it to blend concealer. Use the 402 Setting Brush to dust on highlighter or setting powder. Use the 200 Expert Face Brush to buff liquid and cream foundations- also acts as the perfect tool to create a seamless contour. Miracle Complexion Sponge: Use RT's signature 3-point application technique. Use the flat edge to apply and cover, the round sides to blend and blur, and the precision tip to conceal and perfect. Use damp for a natural, dewy finish.

Cleaning, Care and Maintance

  1. For Brushes: Clean weekly with the Real Techniques Brush Cleansing Gel or Spray for improved brush performance.
  2. For Sponge: Rinse through with lukewarm water after each use. Deep clean once per week with makeup sponge cleanser. Dab on a dot of your favorite makeup sponge cleanser. Add water and gently squeeze to lather. Rinse the sponge with water. Air dry thoroughly in a well-ventilated area. Store in a cool, dry place. Ensure to replace your sponge every 30 uses
